Client Registration Procedure

To keep our cooperation transparent and protect both your commission and the client relationship, client registration is always the first step. Property details, legal documentation, and any further materials are shared with partners only after a client has been registered through the process below.

How it works

  1. Send an email to info@dkg-development.com, or submit the form below, with the client's full name, email, and phone number.
  2. Use the subject line: Registration of a new client — [Name Surname]
  3. Once the registration is confirmed, our team will follow up with the relevant property information, pricing, and documentation for that client.
Email Format

Subject: Registration of a new client – NAME SURNAME

Name: NAME SURNAME

Email: example***@gmail.com

Phone number: +30 698 000 ***

Registering a client through the form below sends the request directly to our partnerships team — no separate email is required.

Kind regards,

DKG Development — Partnerships Team
